Schedule

Please note that this schedule is tentative and may change at any point in time leading up to the event.

All Week Twitter Bikes

Twitter Bikes will allow you to journey around the property on approximately seven miles of bicycle trails that will take you to some of Amelia Island Plantation's points of interest such as Aury Island, Drummond Point Park and Walker's Landing. Plantation trail maps and bikes are available at Amelia's Wheels, just pick one up when you need/want one.

Tuesday May 28th, 2013

Nokia Shuttles from Jacksonville International Airport to Amelia Island

Unless arriving by private jet, Amelia Island is quite remote and that is a very good thing for our purposes. Unfortunately it does make getting here a little complex, but to make it a little easier Nokia will be providing shuttles from the airport on a schedule interval. Details will be out shortly as well as the times the shuttle will run after we have a rough manifest of when everyone is arriving.

CSSConf 9:00AM - 6:00PM

CSSConf is among the first of its kind: a conference dedicated to CSS and the developers and engineers who build the world’s most engaging user interfaces. Rather than focusing on design, this conference focuses on the latest technologies, cutting edge techniques, and tools. Perhaps more importantly, CSSConf will build the kind of community in CSS that JSConf has built around JavaScript, and connect the two communities in a way that has been missing until now.

Microsoft/IE Reach the Beach Welcome Reception TENTATIVELY 6:00PM - 9:00PM

After a long day of travel, come down to the beach for an evening welcome reception and social gathering. Meet old friends, make new friends, and enjoy the sand between your toes! We will have various things going on during this event including light appetizers, refreshments, and possibly a little bit of live music. Help us kick off the JSConf 5th year reunion in true beach style.

Wednesday May 29th, 2013

Significant Others Track - 10:30AM - 5:00PM

If you are traveling with someone that is a ticketed JSConf attendee, we are happy to invite you to a day of non-JS activities! Tour around Amelia Island area to see the highlights and fun adventures that the island has to offer. The track is made possible thanks to our awesome sponsor Condé Nast, but does require registration with a nominal fee opening shortly. It is an incredible time and welcome to significant others of all ages. Full itinerary coming soon.

Thursday May 30th, 2013 - Day of Activities

Bitovi NodeBots Event
9:00AM - 6:00PM

Team up (or go solo) to hack together Arduino based robots and program it all using just JavaScript! If you have no idea what an LED is or a resistor for that matter, have no fear - NodeBot experts will be readily available to help you hardware hack, solder, and 3D print your way to a full fledged NodeBot.

NodeJitsu NodeCopter Event
9:00AM - 6:00PM

Team up (or go solo) to hack a quadcopter using node.js to make a flying robot do your bidding. The NodeCopter event originally launched at JSConf EU and now we take it to the next level at JSConf US 2013. This event will be from 9AM to 6PM.

Poolside with ESRI
9:00AM - 6:00PM

Want to be outside, but still need access to the internet? Come on down to the amazing pooldeck where you can get a nice cold drink, a bit of food, wifi, and, when too hot, jump in the pool or hot tub. Brand new, the pool is the gem of the entire resort - even if you are on other events, you have to stop by the pool for a bit. Who knows, you may even catch a repeat of synchronized.swimming.js.

Beachside with Atlassian
9:00AM - 6:00PM

Hot sun, warm sand, and incredible ocean water - come down to the beach where there will be chairs, cabanas, and umbrellas for you to enjoy. If you get restless, we will have various activities set up including cornhole, volleyball, horseshoes, and bocce ball. Other such fun items (buckets, shovels, etc) will be available and ready for you to craft an epic sand castle or catch a wave!

Github Golf Tournament
Starting at 9:00AM

Take advantage of the world-class Amelia Island golf course overlooking the ocean. The tournament will be organized into teams of four (4) with all teams playing scramble format (AKA best shot of team is taken). If you do not have a team in mind, you will be placed into a team. Golf attire will be required, collared shirts, no jeans. Club rentals are available for $65 or you can bring your own. The golf cart and green fees are sponsored by GitHub.

The Google Segway tour of Amelia Island
Tours run for 2 hours from 9:00AM - 6:00PM

Experience the natural surroundings of the entire Amelia Island Plantation while enjoying the rare chance to ride a Segway! The tour includes a full orientation and guide to introduce you to the island and show you the various sights. Tours last for 2 hours and bottled water will be provided to keep you hydrated.

The Twilio Amazing Scavenger Hunt
Hunts will run from 9:00AM - 6:00PM

Our unique scavenger hunt involves decorating your island hopper (golf cart for four) and setting out to find various clues, brainteasers, and challenges all with the assistance of your smart phone. Be prepared for an epic time while racing to be the first to finish the hunt!

Kayaking with Mozilla down the Amelia River
Tours run for 2 hours from 9:00AM - 6:00PM

Enjoy a unique view of nature and explore Amelia by kayak! A naturalist will lead you through the marsh lands to spot crabs, turtles, birds, and much more! Single and Tandem Kayaks are available. Tours last for 2 hours and bottled water will be provided to keep you hydrated.

R/GA Relax.JS
TENTATIVELY 9:30AM - 2:00PM
